What is BrainLink?

BrainLink is an innovation competition bringing together business and engineering students to solve real problems proposed by partner companies. It encourages creativity, collaboration, and applied learning through teamwork and mentorship.

By creating a bridge between universities and companies, BrainLink provides students with hands-on experience while giving companies access to fresh perspectives and future talent.

The competition consists of two phases where teams develop innovative solutions, receive expert mentorship, and present their ideas to industry leaders.

Collaboration

Teams of business and engineering students working together to solve complex challenges.

Innovation

Fresh perspectives and creative solutions to real industry problems from partner companies.

Excellence

Applied learning and growth through mentorship, resources, and expert guidance.

Real Impact

Industry partnerships that lead to tangible solutions and career opportunities.

Competition Journey

Week 1-2

Registration Opens

Teams register and receive the competition brief with problem statements from our partner company. Kickoff webinar introduces the challenge and rules.

Week 3-5

Preparation Phase

Teams work on their solutions with mentorship sessions, workshops on design thinking and business modeling, and check-ins with advisors.

Week 6

Semifinals

Teams present their innovative solutions to a panel of judges. Top teams are selected to advance to the finals based on creativity, feasibility, and presentation quality.

Week 7-8

Finalist Preparation

Finalist teams refine their solutions with intensive mentorship, prototype development, and presentation coaching from industry experts.

Week 9

Finals & Awards Ceremony

Final presentations to an expert panel including executives from Henkel Algeria. Winners are announced and awarded at a special ceremony with networking opportunities.
